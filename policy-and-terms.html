Availability and Booking:
Slots will be released at 10am on the 9th of every month for the next month. Please note that slot availability may vary depending on the service being booked

Payment and Deposits:
A non-refundable deposit of £10 will be required when applying online for an appointment, this will count towards the total price of your service. Deposits are not required for walk in appointments, however an extra £5 will be added due to the short notice, and you will need to pay the full price upfront after being served.

Service
PLEASE PLEASE SPEAK UP if you do not like how a service is being done during your appointment, as you will not receive a refund if you are dissatisfied at the end of a service. Speak or forever hold your peace. For hair appointments, please bring a reference for the stylist to meet your needs. If requesting a wig construction, please send the hair to the salon AT LEAST 4 DAYS BEFORE YOUR APPOINTMENT to prevent delays.

Arrival and Lateness:
Please be on time to your appointment! for every 15 minutes you are late, a fee of £10 will be charged. after 45 minutes, if you do not turn up, your appointment witll be cancelled.
